AMAZON BEHAVIORAL / LP INTERVIEW QUESTION BANK
Customer Obsession

Tell me about a time when:

You went above and beyond for a customer.

You had to balance competing customer needs.

You had to say no to a customer request and why.

You anticipated a customer’s need before they did.

Ownership

Tell me about a time when:

You took on responsibility beyond your job scope.

You owned a project from start to finish.

You took responsibility for something that went wrong.

Invent & Simplify

Tell me about a time when:

You came up with a simple solution to a complex problem.

You invented something new or improved an existing process.

You simplified a system or process that others thought couldn’t be simplified.

Are Right, A Lot

Tell me about a time when:

You made a decision with incomplete or limited data.

You were wrong about something; what happened and what you learned?

You questioned an assumption that everyone else accepted.

Learn and Be Curious

Tell me about a time when:

You had to learn a new technology quickly.

You taught yourself something to improve your work.

Your curiosity led to a better decision.

Hire and Develop the Best

Tell me about a time when:

You coached or mentored someone effectively.

You helped someone raise their performance.

You had to give difficult feedback to help someone grow.

Insist on the Highest Standards

Tell me about a time when:

You refused to accept poor quality even under pressure.

You raised the bar for your team’s output.

You had to improve a standard that others thought was sufficient.

Think Big

Tell me about a time when:

Your vision led to measurable impact.

You proposed something bold or ambitious.

You went beyond the initial scope of a project.

Bias for Action

Tell me about a time when:

You made a fast decision despite risk.

You took action without full approval.

You solved a problem before anyone asked you to.

Frugality

Tell me about a time when:

You achieved results with limited resources.

You delivered efficiently without extra budget.

You solved a problem without asking for more for more resources.

Earn Trust

Tell me about a time when:

You had to rebuild trust with someone.

You delivered difficult feedback to a peer or manager.

You handled pushback respectfully.

Dive Deep

Tell me about a time when:

You identified a hidden root cause.

You had to dig into data to prove your assumptions.

You encountered conflicting indicators and resolved them.

Have Backbone; Disagree and Commit

Tell me about a time when:

You disagreed with a superior or peer and why.

You convinced others to change direction.

You stood by your analysis despite resistance.

Deliver Results

Tell me about a time when:

You delivered under tight deadlines and adversity.

You had to pivot because your original plan was failing.

You juggled multiple difficult assignments simultaneously.

Additional Real-World Questions (Observed on interview forums)

Tell me about a time you had to negotiate a win-win outcome.

Describe a time when your project failed and what you learned.

Describe how you managed conflicting priorities.

Explain a time when you had to balance speed with accuracy.

Tell me about a time you had to influence without authority.
